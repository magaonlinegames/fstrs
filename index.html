FORSTERS LAWFIRM

The Forsters Firm is dedicated to continuing the lifelong quest of Forster Cochran, whose goal was to bring justice to all. To further that goal in the waning years of his life, he brought together The Forsters Firm, an alliance of Personal Injury lawyers with the resources and the will to press your case against those who have hurt you, no matter who they may be. Once we take your case, we dedicate ourselves to victory for you. Our record of success against some of the most powerful opponents in the country proves our dedication. If you find yourself alone against powerful enemies, The Forsters Firm will stand with you.
